Help Center

Guides and answers for Videh app, calls, and Videh Web.

How do I install Videh on Android?

See Download Videh for step-by-step APK installation.

Calls show “Connecting” but no audio

Ensure both people use the latest app version, accept the call, and have a stable internet connection. Try Wi‑Fi or switch mobile data. Restart the app if needed.

Videh Web will not link

Update the phone app, open Linked devices, and scan the QR at web.videh.co.in. Keep the phone unlocked and online.
