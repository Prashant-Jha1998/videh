Videh Web

Reliable messaging on your desktop — link once with your phone and stay in sync.

How to link Videh Web

  1. Install Videh on your Android phone (download).
  2. On your phone: Settings → Linked devices (or similar).
  3. Open web.videh.co.in on your computer.
  4. Scan the QR code shown on the web page with your phone.
  5. Your chats sync — continue conversations on a larger screen.

Supported browsers

Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari on desktop work best. Keep your phone online for real-time sync.
