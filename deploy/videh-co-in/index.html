Get started in minutes

How to start using Videh

Three simple steps from download to your first call.

1

Download & verify

Install the Android app from our download page, open Videh, enter your mobile number, and confirm OTP. Grant microphone and notifications for the best experience.

2

Find your people

Sync contacts to see who is already on Videh. Create groups for family, work, or college. Set a profile photo and about line so friends recognize you instantly.

3

Chat, call & go web

Send messages, share status, or tap the call button. For desktop, open Videh Web and link via QR under Linked devices.

Messaging depth

Chats that feel complete — not limited

Videh supports everything you expect from a world-class messenger and more: edit and delete messages, view-once media, document previews, voice notes with waveform playback, link previews, and pinned conversations.

Groups include admin controls, invite links, member permissions, and optional send policies so only admins broadcast when needed. Starred messages act as your personal notebook across chats.

Included in every chat

  • Photos, videos, documents, voice notes
  • Replies, reactions, forwards, stars
  • Live & static location sharing
  • Contact cards & link previews
  • Scheduled send for reminders
  • Search across conversation history
Calls & presence

Voice and video built for real networks

Calls start from the chat header — one tap for audio, one for video. In-call controls include mute, speakerphone, and Bluetooth headsets. Group calling scales for team catch-ups.

Presence shows when friends are online or typing. You control how much others see via privacy settings, just like you would expect from a premium messenger.

Call experience

  • 1:1 and group voice / video
  • Incoming call UI with accept / decline
  • Call history inside chat threads
  • Network-friendly WebRTC stack
  • Hold, mute, and audio route picker
Security & trust

Your conversations deserve respect
