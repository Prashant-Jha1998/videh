Frequently asked questions

Answers for new users searching for Videh online.

What is Videh?

Videh is a secure messaging application for India. It offers private chat, voice and video calls, status updates, group tools, and Videh Web for desktop — similar to popular messengers, with India-focused features like Khata and SOS safety.

How do I download Videh?

Visit our Download page for the latest Android APK. Install the app, verify your phone number with OTP, and start chatting.

Is Videh Web available?

Yes. Open Videh Web, scan the QR code from your phone’s linked devices screen, and sync messages on your computer.

Is Videh free?

Videh is free to download and use. We do not sell your personal chats for advertising.
