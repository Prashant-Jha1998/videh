Videh

India's fast, secure messaging app — chat, voice & video calls, status stories, broadcasts, group ledger, SOS safety, and more.

About Videh

Videh helps you stay connected with friends, family, and teams through private messaging, real-time calls, and powerful tools built for everyday communication in India.

Features

Everything you need in one secure messenger.

Private & Group Chat

Send text, photos, videos, documents, and voice notes in one-to-one and group conversations with delivery and read receipts.

Voice & Video Calls

Make high-quality voice and video calls directly from chats. Stay connected wherever you are.

Status (Stories)

Share photo and video status updates that disappear after 24 hours. View who watched and reacted to your status.

Videh Web

Use Videh on your computer. Link your phone with a QR code and sync messages on web.videh.co.in.

Broadcast Lists

Send the same message to many contacts at once without creating a group — perfect for announcements.

Group Ledger (Khata)

Track shared expenses and balances inside group chats — split costs and settle up with your group.

SOS Safety

Quick emergency alert feature to notify trusted contacts when you need help.

Scheduled Messages

Schedule messages to be sent later — never forget birthdays, reminders, or important updates.

Real-time Translation

Break language barriers with in-chat translation so you can talk to anyone, anywhere.

Two-Step Verification

Add an extra PIN layer to protect your account from unauthorized access.

Linked Devices

See and manage devices linked to your account. Log out remotely from unknown sessions.

Starred Messages

Bookmark important messages and find them quickly when you need them.

Location Sharing

Share your live or static location in chat so friends can find you easily.

Contacts & Privacy Controls

Find friends on Videh, control last seen, profile photo visibility, and who can contact you.

Secure by Design

Encrypted connections, OTP login, and privacy-focused design. Your data is not sold for advertising.

Themes & Accessibility

Light and dark themes, notification controls, and settings tuned for comfortable daily use.
