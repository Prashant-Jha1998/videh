About Videh

Videh is a modern messenger designed for friends, families, and small teams across India. Whether you are coordinating a wedding, running a neighbourhood group, or staying in touch with family abroad, Videh keeps conversations organized with receipts, media sharing, calls, and optional tools like shared expense tracking — all in one place.

💬

Who is Videh for?

Anyone who wants a reliable WhatsApp-style experience with extra utilities: students, families, small businesses, community admins, and remote teams who need chat plus calls without juggling multiple apps.

🌐

Phone + Web together

Use Videh on your Android phone, then scan a QR code at web.videh.co.in to read and reply from your laptop — messages stay in sync while your phone stays online.

Security & privacy

We built Videh so you control who sees your profile, when you appear online, and how your data is used.

How we protect you

  • Encrypted connections (TLS) for messages, media, and signaling
  • Phone-number OTP login — no passwords to leak
  • Optional two-step PIN for new device registration
  • Block unknown callers and silence calls from non-contacts
  • Per-chat notification and sound controls
  • No selling of message content for third-party advertising

Privacy controls you can change

  • Last seen & online visibility (everyone, contacts, nobody)
  • Profile photo and About visibility
  • Read receipts on or off
  • Who can add you to groups
  • Disappearing messages timer per chat
  • Download your data or request deletion anytime

Frequently asked questions

Answers to the most common questions about Videh.

Is Videh free to use?

Yes. Videh is free to download and use for messaging, calls, and standard features. We may offer optional paid services in the future; any changes will be announced clearly in the app and on this site.

Do I need Videh Web to use the app?

No. Videh Web is optional. Your phone remains the primary device; Web mirrors chats when your phone is online and linked via QR code.

Are my messages end-to-end encrypted?

Videh encrypts data in transit between your device and our servers using industry-standard TLS. We do not read your messages to show advertisements. See our privacy policy for full details on storage and retention.

How do I delete my account and data?

You can request deletion from the app settings or visit our data deletion page. We process verified requests within the timeframe stated in our policy.

Which devices are supported?

Videh for Android is available now. Videh Web works in modern browsers (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari) when linked to your phone. iOS may be offered in a future release.

How does group Khata (ledger) work?

Inside a group chat, members can add expenses, assign who paid, and track balances. Everyone sees the same ledger so settlements stay transparent.
