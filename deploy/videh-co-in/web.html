Videh Web

Use Videh on your laptop or desktop — read and reply to chats while your phone stays linked.

How to link

  1. Open web.videh.co.in in Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari
  2. On your Android phone, open Videh → Settings → Linked devices
  3. Scan the QR code — your chats sync instantly

Open Videh Web

Why use Videh Web?

  • Full keyboard for long messages and work chats
  • Same TLS transport security as the mobile app
  • Voice notes, media, and group chats supported
  • Phone must stay online for sync
