About Videh

Our mission

Videh helps people stay connected through simple, secure messaging — on mobile and on the web. We focus on reliability, privacy, and features that matter in daily Indian life, from group khata to SOS alerts.

What we believe

  • Desktop and mobile should stay in sync without friction.
