Download Videh for Android

Get the latest Videh app on your phone. Free, secure messaging with calls and status.

Install Videh (Android APK)

  • Download the official Videh APK from your invite or release channel.
  • Enable Install unknown apps for your browser or file manager if prompted.
  • Open the APK and tap Install.
  • Launch Videh, enter your mobile number, and verify with OTP.
  • Allow microphone and notifications for calls and messages.

System requirements

  • Android 8.0 or newer recommended
  • Active mobile number for OTP login
  • Internet connection (Wi‑Fi or mobile data)

Also available on desktop

Use Videh Web after linking your phone — no separate download required.
