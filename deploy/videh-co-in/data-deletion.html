Delete your data

How to remove your Videh account and associated personal information.

In the Videh app

  1. Open Settings → Account
  2. Choose Delete my account and confirm with OTP
  3. Your profile, chats on our servers, and linked devices are scheduled for removal per our policy

On Videh Web

You can also start a deletion request at web.videh.co.in/data-deletion if you cannot access the app.

By email

Email support@videh.co.in from the address linked to your account (or include your registered phone number). We verify ownership before deleting data.

Typical processing time: within 30 days unless law requires retention of specific records.

What is deleted

  • Account profile and authentication data
  • Messages and media stored on Videh servers for your account
  • Linked device sessions and push tokens

Messages already delivered to other users’ devices may remain on their phones; we cannot delete copies on devices we do not control.
