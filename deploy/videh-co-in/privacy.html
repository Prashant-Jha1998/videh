Privacy overview

How Videh handles your information. For the complete legal policy, see the link at the bottom.

What we collect

  • Phone number and profile (name, photo, About) you provide
  • Messages, media, and call metadata needed to deliver the service
  • Videh Video: channel handle, display name, bio, logo, cover, links, uploaded videos, thumbnails, titles, tags, comments, likes, subscriptions, and watch activity
  • Device information (model, OS version) for security and troubleshooting
  • Optional contacts (with your permission) to find friends on Videh
  • Optional location when you share it or use SOS

How we use data

  • To deliver chats, calls, status, notifications, and sync with Videh Web
  • To host Videh Video channels, show recommendations, and measure views and engagement
  • To review reported videos and enforce community standards
  • To deliver optional video ads and measure impressions (advertisers do not receive your private chats)
  • To prevent abuse, spam, and unauthorized account access
  • To improve reliability and fix bugs — we do not sell private message content for ads

Your controls

  • Last seen, profile visibility, read receipts, and group-add permissions
  • Block users and silence unknown callers
  • Disappearing messages and per-chat notification settings
  • Export or delete your data — see data deletion

Videh Video privacy

  • Public channels, uploaded videos, comments, and subscriber counts are visible to other users
  • Your phone number is not shown on public video pages — your @channel handle is used instead
  • Watch history and likes help rank your feed; they are separate from private chat content
  • You can delete videos you uploaded and request full account deletion at any time

Full privacy policy

The detailed policy used in the Videh Android app includes messaging, calls, and Videh Video. Read it in the app under Settings → Help → Privacy Policy, or contact us for a copy.
