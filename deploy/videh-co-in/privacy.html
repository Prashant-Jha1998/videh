Privacy overview

How Videh handles your information. For the complete legal policy, see the link at the bottom.

What we collect

  • Phone number and profile (name, photo, About) you provide
  • Messages, media, and call metadata needed to deliver the service
  • Device information (model, OS version) for security and troubleshooting
  • Optional location when you share it or use SOS

How we use data

  • To deliver chats, calls, status, notifications, and sync with Videh Web
  • To prevent abuse, spam, and unauthorized account access
  • To improve reliability and fix bugs — not to sell message content for ads

Your controls

  • Last seen, profile visibility, read receipts, and group-add permissions
  • Block users and silence unknown callers
  • Disappearing messages and per-chat notification settings
  • Export or delete your data — see data deletion

Full privacy policy

The detailed policy used in the app and for regulatory compliance is hosted at: web.videh.co.in/privacy-policy
